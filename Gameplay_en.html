Player's actions

With Zephyr, you will be propelled into a rogue-lite world. Your skills will be tested in many levels. You'll have to kill all the enemies on your path. In this purpose, your weapon (and your talent), will be of a great help to you. Be careful not to be hit by your enemies. You could die from it and then you'll have to restart again (no wow...). But don't worry, you have a dash. This skill allows you to dodge all your enemies' bullets for a short time. Finally, you also have a (impressive) special skill that gives you a significant advantage over your enemies. But we will come back to it :)



Movement

 You enjoy a magnificent aerial view. On the one hand, to contemplate the beauty of your character but also to have a strategic vision on the actions of your enemies. Choose your movements wisely because they will determine your destiny!!!!

Commands:

-"Z" : move forkward

-"Q": move left

-"S" : move backward

-"D": move right



Shot

 In addition to moving you can.... SHOOT (yes, we know, we tried to be pretty innovative about this). Therefore, aim well.... That's all.

Command:

"[LEFT CLICK]"



Dash

 Good players (like you:D) don't take damages. But you're lucky, we (nice developers, yes we are) have decided to provide you with a skill to help you in this task. This skill is the dash! Thanks to it you'll be able to jump over all enemy fire. Enjoy it (but sparingly) :D

Command:

"[SPACE]"



Special capacity

A character's special skill is a very strong skill. This way you will be able to, for example

Full Care: Bring your character back to full life

Invincible: Make your character invincible for 2s

Shockwave: Launch a devastating shock wave around your character

The Wall: Create a wall in front of you for which you can shelter there

But obviously it takes time to load. Through the enemies killed, past levels and experience gained, your special skill will increase in efficiency<

Command:

"A"

Character's choice

At the beginning of your fabulous adventure, you will have a choice of several characters. Choose carefully this character because it will guide your whole adventure. Indeed, each of them has a skill and an initial weapon of his own. To prevent you from abusing (yes we know you a little anyway ...), we put a cooldown between each use. See this as a child safety.

If you don't like this weapon. No problem! You will find others trow out levels (either in the stores or in some chests). In addition, your weapon improves with each level, as does your special skill.

    The monsters

    You do not look very excited for now ... So to add some challenge to the game, we will add monsters, lots of monsters! You have 5 families of monsters for a total of 11 different enemies. Each family has its own characteristics and behaviors.

    Through your adventures you will be able to face:

  • Zombies

    This is the basic monster, it can only attack in hand-to-hand combat. It has two different forms:

    -Simple zombie

    -Boss Zombie



  • Snow men

    He attacks from a distance, which makes him particularly threatening. It has two different forms:

    -Simple snow man

    -Boss snow man



  • Aliens

    This is one of the most dreadful groups. It has two shooting modes, one normal and the other in zone. It has two different forms:

    -Simple alien

    -Boss alien



  • Evil magicians

    He has a very special mechanics that makes him dreadful. In addition to moving very quickly, at his death, he divides into 2 "Adults" who themselves divides into 2 "babies"

    -Baby magician

    -Adult magician

    -Boss magician



  • Gnomes

    It is necessary to be wary because this group has snipers. Despite his slow movement speed, he has very high damage. It has two different forms:

    -Simple gnome

Gameplay

With Zephyr, you will be propelled into a rogue-lite world. Your skills will be tested in many levels. You'll have to kill all the enemies on your path. In this purpose, your weapon (and your talent), will be of great help to you. Be careful not to be hitted by your enemies. You could die from it and then you'll have to do EVERYTHING to back again (no wow...). But don't worry, you have a roll. This skill allows you to dodge all your enemies' bullets for a short time. Finally, you also have a (impressive) special skill that gives you a significant advantage over your enemies. But we will come back to it:)

At the beginning of your fabulous adventure, you will have a choice of several characters. Choose carefully this character because it will guide your whole adventure. Indeed, each of them has a skill and an initial weapon of his own.

If you don't like this weapon. No problem! You will find others trow out levels (either in the stores or in some chests). In addition, your weapon improves with each level, as does your special skill.



Movement

 You enjoy a magnificent aerial view. On the one hand, to contemplate the beauty of your character but also to have a strategic vision on the actions of your enemies. Choose your movements wisely because they will determine your destiny!!!!

Commands:

-"Z" : move forkward

-"Q": move left

-"S" : move backward

-"D": move right



Shot

 In addition to moving you can.... SHOT (yes, we know, we tried to be pretty innovative about this). Therefore, aim well.... That's all.

Command:

"[LEFT CLICK]"



Roll

 Good players (like you:D) don't take damage. But you're lucky, we (nice developers, yes we are) have decided to provide you with a skill to help you in this task. This skill is rolling! Thanks to it you'll be able to jump over all enemy fire (but sparingly). Enjoy it: D

Command:

"[SPACE]"



Special capacity

A character's special skill is a very strong skill. This way you will be able to, for example

Full Care: Bring your character back to full life

Invincible: Make your character invincible for 2s

Shockwave: Launch a devastating shock wave around your character

The Wall: Create a wall in front of you for which you can shelter there

But obviously it takes time to load. Through the enemies killed, past levels and experience gained, your special skill will increase in efficiency<

Command:

"A"

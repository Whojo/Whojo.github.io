The team

This project is part of an educational framework proposed by EPITA. However, it is still very important for us and the team is deterimed to deliver a good final product. The name of this project is Zephyr. It is a roguelite, like "Enter the Gungeon". The latter has been developed by Dodge Roll and is appreciated by the whole team. We then pick up several elements of it.

Zéphyr is therefore an action-RPG focusing on exploration in order to bring to the player a new gaming experience. The latter is getting closer to the roguelites in the way of making death necessary to be prepared at best against enemies, in particular by allowing access to purchases facilitating the game, in exchange for resources collected in the stage.

The game is dynamic and nervous and required a high level of concentration for the player, especially in certain phases unsually complex. The increasing difficulty in the levels, and the waves of monsters throw the rooms will test the player's skills.
